My Playlist

I listen to many genres of music, and one of my favorites is a genre called "Midwest emo." Although the genre originated in the Midwest U.S., with cities like Chicago, Illinois, and Kansas City, Missouri—hence the name "Midwest emo"—this doesn't necessarily mean that a song must come from these places to be classified as such. Rather, it is the sound that defines the genre. The sound is easily distinguishable and unique, characterized by its twinkly and dissonant sounds.

This playlist is a collection of Midwest emo music, along with other songs from its subgenres, that I enjoy listening to.
